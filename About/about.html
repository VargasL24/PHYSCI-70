My name is Lukas Vargas, I am 16 years old and a student at Manassas Park High School as well as Governors School at Innovation Park. I have 4 siblings, two older sisters and two younger brothers. My family and my girlfriend mean everything to me, and I hope to make all of them proud. From a young age I had always had a passion for physics. I liked to watch shows on TV that talked about it, though I never understood what they were saying. As I grew up I liked tinkering with things I could find around my house, and I would pretend that I was in a forest surviving using only the tools I was able to craft. Now as a rising Junior I have only grown to love physics and engineering more and more. Although where I live doesnt have too many opportunities for me to get hands-on experience I still find plenty of other ways to keep myself busy outside of tinkering with things at home. At my school i'm part of 12 different clubs, my favorites being Theater, DECA, FBLA, and Peer mentorship. I enjoy helping out in my community and have been doing community service since I was 6 years old!
