Final Project

Pitch

My idea for my final project is to make a rocket stable durring flight using MEMS-based rocket guidence systems. What this means is that a small anolog gyroscope and accelerometer would go inside of a small model rocket and make sure it flys straight and steady. The way I plan on making this work is by using a MPU 6050 as the gyroscope and accelerometer, and send that data to an arduino nano. This is going to be the most time consuming part of the project and will be taking up most of my time. I belive that this will take somewhere between 1-3 weeks depending on how much work is done outside of class. The rocket assembily will begin after it arrives from amazon, and i belive that all assembily could get done in 1 weeks lab. The test launches will take place either on weekends or possibly under supervision during lab time in 2-3 weeks. I do not know if I need to 3D print anything at the time, It all depends on how the servos will connect to the fins that come in the packaging. My plan is to program an arduino to recive an input from the gyroscope and accordingly rotate the servos to ajust for the change in directory. For example if the rocket is leaning left 40 degrees, the fins would rotate to the right 40 degrees plus 10% in order to move it back into the correct trijectory.
